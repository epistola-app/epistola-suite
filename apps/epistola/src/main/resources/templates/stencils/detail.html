Upgrading creates a draft — you must publish it yourself

Upgrading a published template lands the change in a new draft; its live version stays unchanged until you open that template and publish the draft (existing drafts are modified in place). The upgrade alone does not change what the template renders, and templates that are deployed to an environment also need to be published to that environment again. Up to 100 templates can be upgraded at a time.
